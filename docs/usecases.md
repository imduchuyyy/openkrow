# OpenKrow Use Cases

## For students
Learning new topics: OpenKrow can help students learn new topics by providing explanations, answering questions, and offering resources for further study.

Input knowledge and information into notebookllm: Students can use notebookllm to store notes, documents, and other types of information that they can access when needed.

## For office workers
Automating office tasks: OpenKrow can help office workers automate tasks such as constructing documents, managing Google Sheets, sending emails, and scheduling calendar events.

## For researchers
Conducting research: OpenKrow can assist researchers in gathering information, conducting research, and providing insights on various topics.
